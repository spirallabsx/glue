Teresa Towey

What we did

Custom web design and development + marketing strategy consulting for a health & wellness coaching business.

Teresa Towey runs a successful coaching business, and needed a cohesive digital presence for prospective client visitors from various sources. We migrated an old website from Wordpress to Wix and built a custom design and template to manage content. The upgrade allows Teresa to seamlessly manage her web content, domain, and hosting under one platform without increasing costs.

‍

We developed a digital marketing strategy and delivered research + recommendations for available lead generation platforms. In addition, we worked with the client on creative direction, production, and editing of a brand-awareness video.
